Design Styles Odyssey

A Journey Through Visual Design Philosophies

Minimalist

Less is more

Focuses on simplicity, clean lines, and essential elements. Removes unnecessary complexity to create clarity and purpose.

Brutalist

Raw, bold, unapologetic

Embraces raw aesthetics, bold typography, and stark layouts. Prioritizes content and function over decorative elements.

Skeuomorphic

Realistic, textured design

Mimics real-world objects with detailed textures and shadows. Designs that look and feel like physical objects.

Flat Design

Clean, two-dimensional

Eliminates shadows, gradients, and three-dimensional effects. Focuses on clean, simple, and crisp two-dimensional interfaces.

Material

Google's design language

Inspired by physical materials, with layering, depth, and meaningful motion. Developed by Google to create consistent user experiences.

Neumorphic

Soft, embossed look

Creates soft UI elements that appear to extrude from the background. Combines flat design with subtle, soft shadows for a unique 3D effect.
